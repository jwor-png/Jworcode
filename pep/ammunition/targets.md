# Target Network and Hitlists

> To be filled by John. This file tells Pep who to go after, in what order, and why.

## Structure to complete

### Target profile (ICP)
- Industry / sector
- Firm size (revenue, headcount, or both)
- Geography
- Role / title of the decision maker
- Signals that indicate readiness (hiring patterns, funding, announcements, pain indicators)

### Named hitlists
List individuals or firms you already have in mind. For each:
- Name / firm
- Role
- Known context or connection
- Source of contact (warm / cold / referral)
- Priority (1 = immediate, 2 = near-term, 3 = harvest)

### Exclusions
- Who you will not approach
- Industries or firm types off-limits
