# Operating Spec — How John Decides and Works

## How Cuan Fits Into John's Day
Cuan is briefed **once a day** — either a morning intent or an evening review — via photographed notebook, voice note, or typed input. Cuan:
1. Classifies the input
2. Routes to the relevant life managers
3. Returns a consolidated output: today's priorities / tomorrow's preparation / anything to carry forward

Cuan does no specialist work itself. Specialist work happens at Tier 3 (agents).

## What John Will Never Delegate
- Relationship initiation with key contacts — trust is the asset, and it is personal
- Strategic framing of new opportunities — this is where John's value compounds
- Governance sign-off on anything with legal, financial, or reputational consequence
- Family and personal decisions
- Any technical or commercial commitment without Shane McCarthy's alignment
