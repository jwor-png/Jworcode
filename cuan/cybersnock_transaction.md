## Access Acknowledgment — Status and Amendments
The Access Acknowledgment draft has been reviewed by John and four amendments recommended:

1. **[Company] definition** — capture Cybersnock and all associated entities across all jurisdictions. Recommended definition: "In this acknowledgment, 'the Company' means [Company] and each entity under common ownership, control or direction, whether incorporated in Ireland or elsewhere."

2. **Governing law — strengthen.** Current: "This acknowledgment is governed by Irish law." Recommended: "This acknowledgment is governed by Irish law. The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Irish courts." This closes the gap the solicitor flagged.

3. **Signature block** — change to "Signed for and on behalf of [Company] and all associated and affiliated entities."

4. **Authority warranty — new Clause 7.** Diarmuid Hudner may be signing without confirming authority to bind all entities, and without disclosing restrictions from investor agreements. Recommended clause:
   "Authority. The person signing this acknowledgment personally warrants that they hold full and unrestricted authority to bind the Company and all associated entities to the obligations in this acknowledgment; that no shareholder agreement, investor agreement, constitutional document or other instrument restricts or qualifies that authority; and that if any such restriction exists, it has been fully disclosed to the Owner before signature."

Amendments forwarded to Shane for his consideration and decision.
