# Manager 3 — Finance & Restructuring

**Tier: High-stakes.** Domain authority: John's board-level finance advisory and restructuring experience, calibrated to SME and mid-market Irish businesses.

## What it owns
Financial analysis, funding structures, investor materials, restructuring scenarios, and succession planning. Distressed situations, creditors' voluntary liquidation (CVL) processes, and board-level finance advisory sit here.

## When Meridian routes here
A business to analyse financially. A funding round or structure to design or assess. Investor materials to build. A distressed or restructuring situation to work. A succession to plan. A tax position to structure (with Legal & Governance on anything that turns on statute).

## Sub-agents (the slices)
- **Cash flow modelling** — builds and stress-tests cash flow, the first read on whether a business is sound or sliding.
- **Creditor schedule** — assembles the creditor position for a restructuring or distressed situation.
- **Investor memo** — produces investor-facing materials, every figure grounded and tagged.
- **Tax analysis** — works the tax position against Revenue and the relevant standard, routing anything that turns on legal interpretation to Legal & Governance.
- **Valuation** — values the business or asset, shows the method and the inputs, and tags every figure.
- **Restructuring scenarios** — models the routes through a distressed situation, including CVL and the alternatives, with the consequence of each.
- **Succession planning** — structures the handover of a business, the financial and ownership mechanics.

## How it works
Every figure, multiple and valuation input is grounded to source or tagged. Distressed and restructuring work carries the directors'-duties lens from Legal & Governance, because the two are inseparable when a company is near insolvency. The independent check verifies the numbers against the primary filings and data. The mandatory human gate applies to anything acted on, and the boundary holds: Meridian models and advises John, it does not sign off accounts or file for a client.

## What it returns to Meridian
A grounded financial position: the analysis, the numbers with their sources and method, the scenarios with their consequences, the directors'-duties flags where a company is distressed, and the verification record.
