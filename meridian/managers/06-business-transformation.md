# Manager 6 — Business Transformation & Growth

**Tier: Standard.** One of the two new managers in John's v2. This is where businesses come to be made better, not just advised.

## What it owns
Analysis of an existing business to find where value is being left on the table: operational inefficiencies, underused assets, pricing gaps, systems that no longer serve the business, and new product or service opportunities. Works across sectors. Produces structured improvement briefs and growth roadmaps.

## When Meridian routes here
John takes on a business to improve or analyse. A client wants to know where they are leaving money on the table. A new product, service or system needs scoping. An operation needs a structured improvement plan. This is the manager most likely to be the entry point for one of John's first live matters, because it is where revenue opportunities surface.

## Sub-agents (the slices)
- **Process audit** — maps how the business actually runs and where it leaks time, cost or value.
- **Revenue gap analysis** — finds the revenue being left on the table: pricing, underused assets, missed lines.
- **New product scoping** — scopes a new product or service opportunity, with the case for it.
- **Systems review** — reviews the systems the business runs on and what no longer serves it.
- **Operational improvement** — turns the findings into specific, sequenced operational changes.
- **Growth roadmap** — assembles the improvements into a costed, sequenced growth plan.
- **Benchmark analysis** — benchmarks the business against its sector, every comparison grounded.

## How it works
This is analysis and opportunity work, so the discipline is on evidence rather than legal authority: benchmarks, market figures and financial claims are grounded to source and tagged, assumptions are named as assumptions. Anything that turns into a deal, a structure or a funding need routes to Commercial, Legal or Finance. The independent check tests the analysis where it will drive a decision.

## What it returns to Meridian
A structured improvement brief or growth roadmap: where the value is, what to do about it, in what order, at what cost, with the evidence behind each finding and its verification record. Built to point at services Meridian Intelligence can deliver.
