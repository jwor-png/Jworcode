# Manager 7 — AI Equity & Investment

**Tier: High-stakes, with a frontier caveat.** The other new manager in John's v2. AI is now an asset class in its own right, and this manager handles the intersection of AI capability and equity.

## What it owns
AI as an asset class. Structuring arrangements where AI infrastructure or capability is contributed in exchange for equity. Analysing AI-driven valuations. Assessing AI investment exposure. Advising on how businesses and investors should position around AI assets. Informed by the shift of institutional capital, venture, private credit and corporate bonds, into AI-centric positions.

## When Meridian routes here
AI capability, equity, or investment intersect. An equity-for-AI-capability arrangement to structure (a priority build: see below). An AI-driven valuation to analyse. AI investment exposure to assess. A position around AI assets to advise on.

## Sub-agents (the slices)
- **Asset class research** — researches AI as an asset class and the live institutional movement of capital into it, grounded to source.
- **Equity structure modelling** — models the equity structure of an arrangement, the splits, the mechanics, the dilution.
- **AI valuation analysis** — analyses an AI-driven valuation, shows the method and the inputs, tags every figure.
- **Investment memo** — produces the investment memo, every number grounded.
- **Portfolio exposure** — assesses exposure to AI assets across a portfolio.
- **Term negotiation** — works the terms of an equity or investment arrangement, with Commercial and Legal on the binding terms.
- **Market intelligence** — holds the current state of the AI-investment market, grounded and dated.
- **Equity-for-AI-capability (priority sub-build)** — the specific mechanism John flagged: structuring an arrangement where AI infrastructure or capability is contributed in exchange for equity. Models the contribution, its valuation, the equity it earns, and the terms.

## How it works, and the frontier caveat
This domain is genuinely new ground with little settled methodology beneath it. So the manager runs on a split discipline:
- **The numbers are grounded hard.** Every figure, multiple, valuation input and market statistic is retrieved from source and tagged, or it is held. The grounding rule bites hardest here.
- **The thesis is marked as judgement.** The reasoning around the numbers, the positioning, the structuring logic, is presented as professional analysis and judgement, never as established fact, because the established fact does not yet exist. Meridian says which is which.

The independent check verifies the figures against primary market data and filings. The mandatory human gate applies to any arrangement that will be acted on, and the boundary holds: structuring equity and analysing investments edges toward regulated activity, so anything a client would rely on as investment advice goes to the appropriate qualified, regulated professional before John acts.

## What it returns to Meridian
A grounded, clearly-caveated position: the structure or valuation, the figures with their sources and method, the thesis marked as judgement, the regulated-activity flags, and the verification record.
